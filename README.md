# Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis — Tableau Dashboard #

Interactive dashboard built in Tableau Desktop Public Edition, analyzing 2017 order data across Category, Sub-Category, Region, and Segment for a retail superstore dataset.

## Tools Used
Tableau Desktop Public Edition

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales: 733.22K
- Profit: ₹0.09M
- Profit Margin: 12.74%
- Return Rate: 6.22%
- Category split — Technology: 54.24%, Office Supplies: 42.53%, Furniture: 3.23%
- Segment split — Consumer: 48.77%, Corporate: 28.66%, Home Office: 22.57%

## Key Findings

Furniture contributes only 3.23% of profit against Technology's 54.24%, and within it, Tables and Bookcases are actually losing money (-8.71% and -0.62%). Copiers are the strongest sub-category at 26.79% profit, driven largely by one product, the Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier (₹15,680 profit alone). Consumer leads segments at 48.77% sales, with Home Office smallest at 22.57%. The profit trend spikes in March (₹14.75K) then drops sharply in May (₹0.93K), worth digging into further.

## Business Recommendation
Revisit pricing/discounts on Tables and Bookcases, they're dragging Furniture into negative profit. Copiers and Technology overall are the better bet for continued investment.

## Filters Available
Region, Year of Order Date, and a metric toggle (Sales / Profit / #Order).

## Dataset
Sample Superstore dataset, filtered to 2017 order data.
